  • Pinnacle odds at each snapshot (time series table)

Extract from TOS:

SECTION 2 — CORE VALUE RULE

RULE A: Pinnacle odds LOWER than market = Pinnacle is hedging = sharp money received = VALUE on that selection.
RULE B: Pinnacle odds HIGHER than market on a selection = Pinnacle is NOT hedging = value in the OPPOSITE selection.

This rule applies identically to 1X2 and Over/Under 2.5.

SECTION 3 — TOS PARAMETERS — DEFINITIONS AND READING RULES

bookImpDiff

  • Formula: book% − Implied%
  • book% = Sel Vol / Tot Vol
  • Implied% = 100 / bookmaker odds
  • POSITIVE = more real money than probability suggests = sharp signal on that selection
  • NEGATIVE = market rejects that selection
bookImpDiff is the PRIMARY directional signal. When it conflicts with bookDiffToNow, bookImpDiff wins.

bookDiffToNow

  • Measures strength and timing of monetary pressure
  • POSITIVE (blue/azzurro) = active sharp money pushing odds down
  • NEGATIVE = market pressure absent or reversed
  • Threshold for strong signal: > +20
bookDiffToNow is SECONDARY — it measures FORCE and TIMING, not direction when conflicting with bookImpDiff.

impDifToNow

  • = 0 constant → odds not yet corrected → MAXIMUM residual value
  • Low and stable → minimal correction → value still present
  • Rising → bookmaker correcting → value eroding
  • Negative → market pushing back against signal
Best entry condition: impDifToNow = 0 + bookImpDiff high and stable = ideal entry.

Sel Vol

  • Volume traded on that selection on Betfair exchange
  • Sel Vol rising + odds falling = Back money = signal confirmed
  • Sel Vol rising + odds rising = Lay money = signal reversed

Tot Vol

  • Total volume on entire market — SAME for all selections — do NOT use to compare between Home/Draw/Away

PinnyPrice

  • PinnyPrice < market = Pinnacle hedging = value on that selection
  • PinnyPrice aligned to market = no outlier

SECTION 4 — OUTLIER DETECTION (AsianOdds)

How to detect:

  1. Read Pinnacle row: Home / Draw / Away odds
  2. Compare each value against the average of all other bookmakers
  3. Calculate gap: (Pinnacle − Market avg) / Market avg × 100 = gap%
Gap%Outlier strength
> 5%Weak outlier
> 10%Moderate outlier
> 15%Strong outlier
> 20%Very strong outlier
  • Pinnacle LOWER than market = Pinnacle hedged = VALUE on that selection
  • Pinnacle HIGHER than market = Pinnacle not hedged = value in OPPOSITE selection
Outlier absent: Pinnacle aligned to market on all three selections = no outlier signal.

SECTION 5 — ODDS MOVEMENT READING (AsianOdds time series)

Read snapshot table: oldest at bottom, most recent at top.

  • Home odds rising over time = money on Away/Draw
  • Away odds falling over time = sharp signal on Away
  • Draw odds stable while Home/Away move = indecision on X

Meaningful movement: > 0.10 on Pinnacle over full period is significant.
Late movement (last 2-3 snapshots): Weight ×2 — more informative than early movement.

Four main indicators:

  1. bookImpDiff (TOS)
  2. PinnyPrice vs market (TOS or AsianOdds outlier)
  3. Sel Vol dominance (TOS)
  4. bookDiffToNow > +20 (TOS)

SECTION 7 — bookDiffToNow PATTERNS (A–H)

PATTERN A — Late explosion

  • bookDiffToNow starts low/negative, then jumps > +20 in last 2-3 hours. Reliability: HIGH.
Rule: bookDiffToNow > +20 late + impDifToNow = 0 → maximum quality signal

PATTERN B — High and stable all day

  • Values > +20 constant throughout day. Reliability: HIGH.

PATTERN C — Starts high then falls toward 0

  • Bookmaker corrected odds. Original signal was real. Reliability: MEDIUM-HIGH.
Rule: Respect original signal if bookImpDiff remains positive even if bookDiffToNow erodes.

PATTERN D — Inverts from positive to negative

  • Original signal absorbed and reversed.
HARD STOP: bookDiffToNow inverts negative → DO NOT BET. Applies regardless of all other signals.

PATTERN E — Stable negative

  • Market rejects that selection. Exclusion filter only — not directional signal for opposite.

PATTERN F — Progressive growth during day

  • Distributed accumulation of sharp money. Reliability: HIGH.

PATTERN G — Inversion from negative to strongly positive

  • Total signal reversal — massive late entry overwrites everything. Reliability: VERY HIGH.

PATTERN H — Draw bookDiffToNow very negative

  • Market does not believe in Draw = match will be decided. Does NOT say who wins. Use as exclusion filter for Draw.

SECTION 8 — SPECIAL FILTERS

Playoff / aggregate filter

Return legs with aggregate tied or ±1 goal: raise convergence threshold to 4/4 before betting Under.

Lay rule

bookImpDiff positive + Sel Vol dominant on non-Home (X or 2) → lay Home on Betfair covers two scenarios.

False signal filters — do NOT bet if:

  • Isolated spike on AsianOdds without TOS confirmation
  • bookImpDiff positive but PinnyPrice aligned to market
  • bookDiffToNow not positive despite bookImpDiff positive
  • All movement in first 2 days with no recent confirmation = stale signal
  • Sel Vol rising + odds rising = verify if Lay

SECTION 9 — H2H HISTORICAL HYPOTHESIS (IN VALIDATION — NOT YET OPERATIONAL)

  • Historical avg Home odds > current odds = value hypothesis on Home
  • Historical avg Home odds < current odds = value hypothesis on Away/Draw
⚠️ Data collection phase only. Do NOT use as standalone signal.

SECTION 10 — PRECEDENCE RULES SUMMARY

  1. bookImpDiff prevails over bookDiffToNow when they conflict
  2. Late movement (last 2-3 hours) weighs ×2
  3. AsianOdds outlier > +15% = strong standalone signal even without TOS
  4. Pattern D (inversion to negative) = hard stop regardless of all other signals
  5. Pinnacle higher than ALL bookmakers on a selection = very strong signal on opposite

Rules for filling the VERDICT:

  • If 1X2 and O/U both have signals → show both as Primary and Secondary
  • If TOS and AsianOdds conflict → write CONFLITTI and apply precedence rule (bookImpDiff wins over bookDiffToNow; AsianOdds outlier >15% is strong standalone)
  • If TOS not available → write "ASPETTA TOS" only if match is still hours away and TOS data could change verdict; otherwise proceed with MODERATA quality
  • SKIP only if: convergences 1/4, or Pattern D active, or no outlier and no TOS
  • GIOCA if: convergences 3/4 or 4/4, or strong outlier (>15%) confirmed by movement
  • Stake: 0.50u = Moderata, 1.00u = Forte, 2.00u = Molto forte
