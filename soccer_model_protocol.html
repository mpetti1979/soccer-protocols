SECTION 2 — CORE VALUE RULE

RULE A: Pinnacle odds LOWER than market = Pinnacle is hedging = sharp money received = VALUE on that selection.
RULE B: Pinnacle odds HIGHER than market on a selection = Pinnacle is NOT hedging = value in the OPPOSITE selection.

This rule applies identically to 1X2 and Over/Under 2.5.

SECTION 3 — TOS PARAMETERS — DEFINITIONS AND READING RULES

bookImpDiff

  • Formula: book% − Implied%
  • book% = Sel Vol / Tot Vol
  • Implied% = 100 / bookmaker odds
  • POSITIVE = more real money than probability suggests = sharp signal on that selection
  • NEGATIVE = market rejects that selection
bookImpDiff is the PRIMARY directional signal. When it conflicts with bookDiffToNow, bookImpDiff wins.

bookDiffToNow

  • Measures strength and timing of monetary pressure
  • POSITIVE (blue/azzurro) = active sharp money pushing odds down
  • NEGATIVE = market pressure absent or reversed
  • Threshold for strong signal: > +20
bookDiffToNow is SECONDARY — it measures FORCE and TIMING, not direction when conflicting with bookImpDiff.

impDifToNow

  • Measures how much the bookmaker has already corrected the odds
  • = 0 constant → odds not yet corrected → MAXIMUM residual value
  • Low and stable → minimal correction → value still present
  • Rising → bookmaker correcting → value eroding
  • Negative → market pushing back against signal
Best entry condition: impDifToNow = 0 + bookImpDiff high and stable = odds not yet corrected = ideal entry.

Sel Vol

  • Volume traded on that selection on Betfair exchange
  • Read in combination with: (1) absolute value, (2) timing — right side of chart = recent = more valuable, (3) direction vs odds movement
  • Sel Vol rising + odds falling = Back money = signal confirmed
  • Sel Vol rising + odds rising = Lay money = signal reversed

Tot Vol

  • Total volume on entire market (1X2 = sum of 1+X+2)
  • SAME for all selections — do NOT use to compare between Home/Draw/Away

PinnyPrice

  • Pinnacle current price on TOS
  • PinnyPrice < market = Pinnacle hedging = value on that selection
  • PinnyPrice aligned to market = no outlier

SECTION 4 — OUTLIER DETECTION (AsianOdds)

Definition: An outlier exists when Pinnacle odds differ significantly from the market consensus.

How to detect from AsianOdds screenshot:

  1. Read Pinnacle row: Home / Draw / Away odds
  2. Compare each value against the average of all other bookmakers
  3. Calculate gap: (Pinnacle − Market avg) / Market avg × 100 = gap%
Gap%Outlier strength
> 5%Weak outlier
> 10%Moderate outlier
> 15%Strong outlier
> 20%Very strong outlier

Interpretation:

  • Pinnacle LOWER than market by X% = Pinnacle hedged = sharp money on that selection = VALUE there
  • Pinnacle HIGHER than market by X% = Pinnacle not hedged = value in OPPOSITE selection
Outlier absent: When Pinnacle is aligned to market on all three selections simultaneously = no outlier signal.

Example — Progreso vs Peñarol (04/04/2026):

  • Pinnacle Draw: 4.15 vs bet365: 3.50 → Pinnacle much higher on Draw → NOT hedging Draw → value on Home or Away
  • Pinnacle Away: 1.47 vs market ~1.44 → Pinnacle slightly higher → mild signal against Away
  • Conclusion: Pinnacle not hedging Draw or Away → signal toward decisive result (Home or Away win)

SECTION 5 — ODDS MOVEMENT READING (AsianOdds time series)

Read the snapshot table chronologically: oldest at bottom, most recent at top.

Direction signals:

  • Home odds rising over time = money on Away/Draw
  • Away odds falling over time = money on Away = sharp signal on Away
  • Draw odds stable while Home/Away move = indecision on X

Meaningful movement: A move of > 0.10 on Pinnacle over the full period is significant.
Late movement (last 2-3 snapshots before kickoff): Weight ×2 — late sharp money is more informative than early movement.

SECTION 7 — bookDiffToNow PATTERNS (A–H)

PATTERN A — Late explosion

  • Form: bookDiffToNow starts low or negative, then sharp jump to > +20 in last 2-3 hours before kickoff
  • Interpretation: Massive late sharp money with updated information
  • Reliability: HIGH
Rule: bookDiffToNow > +20 late + impDifToNow = 0 → maximum quality signal

PATTERN B — High and stable all day

  • Form: Values > +20 constant throughout the day
  • Interpretation: Constant persistent monetary pressure — money entering gradually
  • Reliability: HIGH
Rule: bookDiffToNow > +20 stable + impDifToNow low = confirmed constant pressure

PATTERN C — Starts high then falls toward 0

  • Form: Starts +20/+40, progressively falls toward 0 near kickoff
  • Interpretation: Bookmaker reacted and corrected odds, absorbed pressure. Original signal was real.
  • Reliability: MEDIUM-HIGH
Rule: Even if bookDiffToNow erodes, respect original signal if bookImpDiff remains positive

PATTERN D — Inverts from positive to negative

  • Form: Starts positive, becomes negative near kickoff
  • Interpretation: Original signal absorbed and reversed by market. Selection loses force.
  • Reliability: HIGH as stop signal
HARD STOP: bookDiffToNow inverts negative → DO NOT BET or exit position. Applies regardless of other signals.

PATTERN E — Stable negative on a selection

  • Form: Consistent significant negative values
  • Interpretation: Market rejects that selection
  • Use: Exclusion filter only — not a directional signal for opposite

PATTERN F — Progressive growth during day

  • Form: Starts low positive, grows gradually throughout day
  • Interpretation: Distributed accumulation of sharp money
  • Reliability: HIGH

PATTERN G — Inversion from negative to strongly positive

  • Form: Starts negative, then sharp reversal to very high positive (extreme variant of Pattern A)
  • Examples: Wigan (−15 → +60), Torino (negative → +15 in last hours)
  • Interpretation: Total signal reversal — massive late entry overwrites everything
  • Reliability: VERY HIGH for the late signal

PATTERN H — Draw bookDiffToNow very negative

  • Form: bookDiffToNow on Draw strongly negative and stable
  • Interpretation: Market does not believe in Draw = match will be decided. Does NOT say who wins.
  • Use: Additional filter to identify winner between the two teams

SECTION 8 — SPECIAL FILTERS

Playoff / aggregate filter (European football)

Return legs with aggregate tied or ±1 goal = both teams must attack = O/U signal systematically distorted toward Over.
Rule: Raise convergence threshold to 4/4 before betting Under in these matches.

Lay rule

When bookImpDiff positive + Sel Vol dominant converge on non-Home selection (X or 2) → lay of Home team on Betfair covers two scenarios with one bet.

False signal filters — do NOT bet if:

  • Isolated spike on AsianOdds without TOS confirmation
  • bookImpDiff positive but PinnyPrice aligned to market = adjustment only, not outlier
  • bookDiffToNow not positive despite bookImpDiff positive = pressure not confirmed
  • All movement happened in first 2 days with no recent confirmation = stale signal
  • Sel Vol rising + odds rising = verify if Lay before concluding

SECTION 9 — H2H HISTORICAL HYPOTHESIS (IN VALIDATION — NOT YET OPERATIONAL)

  • Historical average Home odds > current odds = forecast > market = value hypothesis on Home
  • Historical average Home odds < current odds = forecast < market = value hypothesis on Away/Draw
⚠️ Data collection phase only. Do NOT use as standalone signal until further notice.

SECTION 10 — PRECEDENCE RULES SUMMARY

  1. bookImpDiff prevails over bookDiffToNow when they conflict
  2. Late movement (last 2-3 hours before kickoff) weighs ×2
  3. AsianOdds outlier > +15% = strong standalone signal even without TOS
  4. Pattern D (inversion to negative) = hard stop regardless of all other signals
  5. Pinnacle higher than ALL bookmakers on a selection = very strong signal on opposite
